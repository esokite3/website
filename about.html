ABOUT ME

I am a second year computer science student at UC San Diego. I went to a performing arts middle school and was in the visual arts academy in high school. Why the pivot to computer science? I have always been enthusiastic about STEM subjects, and when my brother took interest in tech, I decided to give it a try. I participated in the Girls Who Code Summer Immersive Program in 2022 where I felt as though working with CSS closely resembled art. I hope to explore the intersection of technology and the arts through my work in CS.
